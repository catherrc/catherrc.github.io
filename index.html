My goal is to become a legendary creative director.

At USC’s School of Cinematic Arts, I have worked on several short films that have gotten into film festivals. I’ve worked on a Project Involve Film Independent production as an art director, and I was part of AnimTV, an animation club at our school. Now a game director for Hacker at USC Games, my goal is to create the most positive and welcoming team atmosphere that I can, and I try my best to inspire a cooperative team culture.

Who I Am

I am a 3-time NFAA YoungArts Foundation winner (Visual Arts in 2015 and Cinematic Arts in 2016 and 2017), and I have won 8 National Scholastic Art Medals (one of them as a Best in Category American Visions Award for Animation, one portfolio award, and one for video games.) I am also a California Arts Scholar and have attended CSSSA’s Animation program. In 2016, I was Palm Beach and Martin County’s Pathfinder winner for Academic Excellence.
